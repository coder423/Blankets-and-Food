What is Blankets and Food

Blankets and Food is a website that will tell people to give blankets and Food to the poor. .

How will this website help?

This website will help spread awareness of how many people don't get a proper meal everyday and find people to give Food and Blankets to.

Did you know that 26.8 percent of the people in Baltimore starve everyday?

With the help of this website hopefully that number will decrease everyday.

Help people ...

This website will help spread awareness and hopefully people will give out food blankets and other warm accessories for the winter. People should not have to be cold or hungry I hope that one day this website will be used wordwide to help anyone in need.
